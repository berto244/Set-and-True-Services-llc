OUR STORY

Built on the principle of doing it right the first time.

Set and True Services LLC was founded in 2026 right here in the Orlando / Central Florida area with one clear goal: to bring old-school craftsmanship and modern reliability to every home we touch.

The name “Set and True” comes from the trades — it means setting your work up correctly and making sure it’s perfectly true, level, plumb, and square. That’s how we approach every single job, whether it’s a 15-minute repair or a full kitchen renovation.

We’re a locally owned and operated small business. When you hire us, you’re working directly with skilled professionals who care deeply about the outcome and your satisfaction.
